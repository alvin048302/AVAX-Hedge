How the AI Analysis Works
🔍
Step 1 — Live web search
When you tap Analyse, the app calls the Claude AI API with web search enabled. It searches for the latest AVAX price, RSI, MACD, support and resistance levels from multiple financial sources in real time.
📊
Step 2 — TA synthesis
Claude reads the search results and synthesises a multi-timeframe technical picture: trend direction, key levels, momentum indicators, BTC correlation signal.
📐
Step 3 — LP range calculation
Based on identified support and resistance, Claude proposes an S/R-anchored LP range: range low = nearest meaningful support, range high = nearest meaningful resistance. Updates every time you analyse.
⚡
Step 4 — Trade signal
Claude determines long/short/wait based on: LP zone position (upper = long bias, lower = short bias), RSI reading, MACD direction, proximity to key levels, and BTC trend. Gives a confidence % and exact HL order parameters.
Confidence Level Explained
🟢
High (70%+) — Act on signal
Multiple indicators aligned. Open position as instructed with full $1,000 margin at 2×.
🟡
Medium (50–70%) — Reduced size
Mixed signals. If you trade, use $500 margin only. Set tighter stop loss.
🔴
Low (<50%) — Wait
Conflicting signals. No trade tonight. Keep LP running, monitor crash hedge.
LP Range Rules
📏
S/R Anchored (Option B)
Range low = real support level. Range high = real resistance level. Boundaries have market meaning — price is more likely to bounce at these levels, keeping LP in range longer.
🔄
48-Hour Rebalance Rule
Only rebalance when LP has been out of range for 48+ hours AND price has moved more than 10% from centre. Do not chase brief excursions — price often returns.
⚠️
Never rebalance into falling market
If LP exits bottom, wait for price to stabilise for 24–48h before re-entering. Do not catch a falling knife.
