How to Use
Step-by-step guide
What this calculator does
You run two simultaneous positions: a Concentrated Liquidity LP on Blackhole (Avalanche) paired with a directional perpetual hedge on Hyperliquid. Each evening you enter tonight's figures and get exact instructions — whether to hold, flip the hedge direction, or rebalance the LP into a new price range.
Goal: Earn continuous LP fees while hedge profits fund range resets whenever AVAX moves out of band. Never be out of range longer than one evening.
What to key in each night
1
AVAX Price
Check on Hyperliquid or CoinGecko. Enter the current live price. This drives every calculation below it.
2
AVAX in LP and USDC in LP
Open Blackhole, go to your LP position. It shows current token amounts. Enter both. Below range: you will see only AVAX and zero USDC. Above range: only USDC and zero AVAX.
3
Range Low and Range High
Your current LP price boundaries from Blackhole. On first setup use price minus 5.11% for low and price plus 4.87% for high. After each rebalance, enter the new range you just set.
4
LP Entry Price
The AVAX price when you first opened this LP. Used to calculate impermanent loss. Keep the same value until you fully close and reopen the LP.
5
APY
The current APY shown on Blackhole for your pool. Check it weekly and update. It changes with trading volume. Default is 150%.
6
Margin and Leverage
Margin = $1,000 (capital deposited on Hyperliquid). Leverage = 3x. These stay constant — only change if you deliberately resize the hedge.
7
Funding Rate
On Hyperliquid, select AVAX-PERP, look for the Funding rate near the order panel. It shows something like 0.0125% per 8 hours. Positive means shorts earn. Enter it here as a decimal number.
8
Active Side, Size and Entry
Go to Hyperliquid Positions. Select Short or Long. Enter the notional size in USD and the price you opened the trade at. If nothing is open, select None.
9
Unrealised P&L
Optional. Your current open profit or loss shown on Hyperliquid Positions panel. Positive or negative number. Leave at zero if unsure — the calculator estimates it from your entry price.
What the calculator generates
Status banner — In Range (fees running), Below Range (100% AVAX no fees), Above Range (100% USDC no fees), or Warning (approaching boundary).
Range bar — shows where price sits inside your range. Left half means SHORT zone. Right half means LONG zone.
Hyperliquid Orders — exact orders: direction, entry, AVAX size, margin, leverage, Take Profit price, Stop Loss price. Copy directly into Hyperliquid.
Trigger Order — dormant order to set before leaving. When your TP fires, this auto-opens the opposite position without you needing to be online.
Rebalance Box — only appears when LP is out of range. Shows new range, exact AVAX to sell or buy, and what to deposit.
Tonight's Actions — numbered steps for your exact situation tonight. Follow them in order.
Log Rebalance — form appears when out of range. Records the event to Journal with full P&L breakdown.
Key trading rules
SituationWhat to do
Price at or below midpointOpen SHORT
Price above midpointOpen LONG
Short Take Profit levelRange Low minus Range Width
Long Take Profit levelRange High plus Range Width
Short Stop Loss levelRange High minus 20% of width
Long Stop Loss levelRange Low plus 20% of width
When TP firesRebalance LP now
When SL firesFlip to opposite direction
AVAX still fallingDo NOT rebalance yet
Price sideways 48 hoursSafe to rebalance
Most important rule: Never rebalance LP into a still-falling market. Wait for the Short TP to fire first. That is your confirmation that the market has moved far enough. Only then rebalance.
Daily routine
AM
Morning — 5 minutes
Check AVAX price. Confirm LP is in range. Verify TP and SL are set on Hyperliquid. Set trigger order for today's price level. Done until evening.
PM
Evening — 10 to 30 minutes
Enter tonight's figures, tap Calculate, follow action steps in order. In range takes 10 minutes. Out of range with rebalance takes 20 to 30 minutes.
WK
Weekly — 10 minutes
Review Journal tab. Check cumulative P&L and total fees. Update APY if Blackhole shows a new rate. Sync to Google Sheets for backup.
